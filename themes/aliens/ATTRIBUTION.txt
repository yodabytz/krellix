Aliens — krellix theme
======================

The image assets in this directory (bg_panel.png, bg_meter.png,
bg_chart.png, krell_panel.png, krell_meter.png, data_in.png,
data_out.png) are taken from the original GKrellM "Aliens" theme by:

    Alan Swanson <swanson@uklinux.net>

Originally distributed under the GPL alongside GKrellM, ca. 2001.
Source bundle: http://muhri.net/gkrellm/aliens.tgz

The theme.json mapping these assets into krellix's theme format is part
of the krellix project (GPL-3.0-or-later).
